Tomato sauce

Date: 9/02/2025

Ingredients:

2x 400g cans of crushed tomatoes

1x onion

1x carrot

3x clove of garlic

500g spaghetti pasta


Method:

Dice onion

Coin carrot (1cm wide)

Boil carrot coins for 2 minutes in microwave

Strain carrots

Peel garlic

Finely dice garlic

Fill pan with olive oil just enough to not be able to see bottom of pot

Heat up to 6/10

Saute onion for 2 minute

Saute carrot for 1 minute

Saute the diced garlic for 30 seconds

Fill pot with canned crushed tomatoes

Bring up to boil and then boil for five minutes stirring thouroughly

Simmer for 10 minutes

Turn off heat

Use cooking instructions for your spaghetti to cook spaghetti

Blend sauce

Mix sauce with pasta in each bowl

Salt to taste
